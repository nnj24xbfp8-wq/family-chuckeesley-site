Dale Eesley died young, before his marriage to **Thelma** could take place. Thelma stayed close to the family until her own death in the 1970s — counted as family even though no wedding ever happened. The dates of Dale's birth and death are not yet established in this archive.

Listed as a spouse of Thelma here as a convention for keeping the connection visible on both pages; in fact they were engaged, not married.
