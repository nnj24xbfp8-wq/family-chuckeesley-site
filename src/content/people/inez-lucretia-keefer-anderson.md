Born **13 July 1878 in Perry Township, Clarion County, Pennsylvania** — western Pennsylvania's oil and farming country, north of Pittsburgh. Married [John Wallace Anderson](/family/john-wallace-anderson/) on **27 April 1900 in Pittsburgh**; she was 21, he was 35 and had been widowed for four years. Died **21 November 1946 in Oil City, Venango County, Pennsylvania**, age 68 — Oil City being the historic center of the Pennsylvania oil rush that had defined the regional economy since 1859. The **Anderson Farm at Leeper, Clarion County** where John Wallace was buried after her death is near her own Clarion County birthplace, suggesting the family's later-life base was on Inez's home ground.
