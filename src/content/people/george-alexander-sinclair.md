Connected to the Eesley line by his **1899 marriage to [Bessie (Easley) Eesley](/family/bessie-eesley/)** in Toronto — the same year as the [Eesley family group portrait](/archive/eesley-family-group-portrait-1899/) was taken in the city. The Sinclair marriage is the reason the William Eesley obituary lists Bessie as **"Mrs. George Sinclair of Toronto"** rather than under her given name.

Born **9 May 1874 in Ontario**; died **1951 in Toronto**, where he was buried. His own family of origin and trade are queued research.
