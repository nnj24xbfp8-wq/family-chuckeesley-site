**Norma Jean (Wolfe) Grundset** is the **middle daughter** of **[Betty Jean (Davis) Wolfe](/family/betty-davis/)** and **[Norman Graham Wolfe](/family/norman-graham-wolfe/)** &mdash; **named twice over for her parents**:

- **Norma** &mdash; after her father **Norman** Graham Wolfe.
- **Jean** &mdash; after her mother **Betty Jean** Davis Wolfe.

The double-naming pattern is the most explicit family-loyalty naming in the Wolfe daughter trio: she carries both parents' names. She is **Chuck's mother [Terrie Lee Wildermuth](/family/terrie-lee-eesley/)'s first cousin** on the maternal Davis side.

She married **Kenneth Grundset** and was living in **Largo, Pinellas County, Florida** &mdash; the Tampa-Bay-area suburb &mdash; at the time of her parents' 2015 deaths. Norm and Betty had moved to Largo c. 2013 specifically to be near their daughters Norma and Peggy in Florida; **the Florida cousin's house was the gravitational pull for the elder Wolfe migration** in the family's last decade.

Norm died in Largo on 9 January 2015; Betty followed six months and nine days later on 18 July 2015, **her daughters and her favorite grand dog Lily by her side during her final days reminiscing, laughing, and talking about the love and strength of family** (per Betty's obituary). Norma was one of those daughters.
