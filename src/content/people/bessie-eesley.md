Bessie Eesley was one of [Albert Robert Eesley Sr](/family/albert-robert-eesley/) and [Jennie Goldie](/family/jeanie-goldie/)'s ten children, identified by name in the **1899 Eesley family group portrait caption** that Chuck transmitted to this archive in 2026.

She does not appear by name in [William Eesley's c. 1916 obituary](/docs/william-eesley-obituary-college-corner/), which lists the three daughters as Anna (Mrs. J. B. Scoon), Mrs. Jennie Baxter, and Mrs. George Sinclair — all of Toronto. The most parsimonious reading is that **Bessie was Mrs. George Sinclair's given name**, and the obituary's convention of identifying married women only by their husband's name obscured it. An alternative reading is that Bessie was a separate daughter omitted from the obituary's roster.
