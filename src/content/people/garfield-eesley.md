Garfield Eesley was one of [Albert Robert Eesley Sr](/family/albert-robert-eesley/) and [Jennie Goldie](/family/jeanie-goldie/)'s ten children, identified in [William Eesley's c. 1916 obituary](/docs/william-eesley-obituary-college-corner/) as one of the **six Eesley brothers who became millers**. His name is the strongest single dating clue for this generation: **President James A. Garfield was assassinated on 2 July 1881 (dying 19 September 1881)** and the name carried through into widespread American baby-naming for the next several years. **Garfield Eesley was almost certainly born between late 1881 and the late 1880s**, making him one of the youngest of the ten siblings.

He is identified in the **1899 family group portrait** (caption: *"Garfield"*) alongside his siblings.

He is **Chuck's great-great-uncle** on the paternal side.
