Margaret Adelyne Youman was born June 24, 1913 — five years younger than her husband Donald Stuart Eesley. She and Don had five children together (see [Don's page](/family/don-eesley/)). She appears beside Don in the [late-1940s/'60s family group portrait](/archive/eesley-family-group-portrait-late-1940s/) and again in the [c. 1971–1972 Highland Ridge reunion portrait](/archive/highland-ridge-family-group-portrait-c-1980/), where Roberta Burnes's caption distinguishes her from her sister-in-law by the brother she married.

## See also

She is one of [the three Peggys](/docs/the-three-peggys/) &mdash; the other Eesley-side Peggy of the same generation, distinguished in family memory by the husband: *Will's Peggy* and *Don's Peggy.* A short companion essay reads the trio alongside Peggy Arnold of Highland Ridge.

> *Source: Mary Eesley Bean, *[Eesley Family History](/docs/eesley-family-history-1985/)*, p. 8.*
