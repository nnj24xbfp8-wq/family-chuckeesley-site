Margaret Adelyne Youman was born June 24, 1913 — five years younger than her husband Donald Stuart Eesley. She and Don had five children together (see [Don's page](/family/don-eesley/)). She appears beside Don in the [late-1940s/'60s family group portrait](/archive/eesley-family-group-portrait-late-1940s/).

> *Source: Mary Eesley Bean, *[Eesley Family History](/docs/eesley-family-history-1985/)*, p. 8.*
