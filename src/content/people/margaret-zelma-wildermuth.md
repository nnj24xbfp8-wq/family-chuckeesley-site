Born **6 November 1891 in Marietta, Ohio**, the **middle child** of [William Clifford Wildermuth](/family/william-wildermuth/) and [Flora Schlicher](/family/flora-schlicher-wildermuth/), between her elder brother [Charles Daniel](/family/charles-daniel-wildermuth/) and her younger sister [Emma](/family/emma-wildermuth/). Visible in the **[c. 1896 family group portrait](/archive/william-wildermuth-family-group-portrait/)** at about age five — almost certainly the small girl seated front-row, in white, beside the smaller children.

## The California life and a hard 1943

She died **25 October 1943 in Los Angeles**, age **51**, and was buried at **Forest Lawn Memorial Park, Long Beach** — meaning by the end of her life she had moved from Marietta Ohio across the country to southern California. Whether she traveled the same Marietta-to-California route Robert Earl Wildermuth would take a few years later, or arrived via her brother Charles's Kansas relocation, or by some other path, is open research.

Her death came **six weeks after her father William Clifford's** death in Marietta on 10 September 1943 — the daughter dying soon after the father, in the middle of WWII. Both 1943 deaths in the same year are striking; the Marietta Times obituary archive might document either or both.
