Aunt Jeanne is one of the keepers of the family memory this site rests on. Her contributions across this archive include the **oral-history account of [Stella Sunn](/family/stella-sunn/) — how she came to live with the Eesleys** through the wartime journey via Hawaii, the Philippines, and finally Ohio — and a substantial set of the **family photographs** that appear in the artifacts collection. Her brother Charlie's [12th-grade autobiography](/docs/charles-eesley-12th-grade-autobiography-1965/) names her engagement and June 1965 wedding to **Joseph "Joe" Kamiab**; the [Will and Peggy Eesley family portrait with Joe, c. 1965](/archive/eesley-family-with-joe-kamiab-1965/) captures the whole family — Will, Peggy, Charlie, Anne, Maggie, Jeanne herself, and Joe — together at or around that wedding. Her daughter [Stephanie "Stephie" Kamiab](/family/stephanie-kamiab/) is named in passing in Charlie's late-1970 Vietnam letter to Terrie as the small child who already called Terrie "Aunt Terrie" before the wedding.
