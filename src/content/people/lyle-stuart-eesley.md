Lyle Stuart Eesley is the eldest of [Donald Stuart "Don" Eesley](/family/don-eesley/) and [Margaret Adelyne "Peggy" Youman Eesley](/family/peggy-don-eesley/)'s five children — Chuck Eesley's first cousin once removed on the paternal Eesley side. His siblings are Robert Douglas, Marilyn, Charles Stuart, and Donald Steven Eesley. His son is Daniel Stewart Eesley.

## The namesake

Lyle Stuart Eesley was named for his uncle [Lyle Eesley](/family/lyle-eesley/), one of [Charles Leonard Eesley](/family/charles-leonard-eesley/)'s eight children — Don's brother — who died on 25 July 1942 at the **Cabanatuan Prison Camp** on Luzon in the Philippines, almost certainly a Bataan Death March survivor who did not survive the camp. The elder Lyle had been twenty-five years old. Lyle Stuart was born six years after his uncle's death; the family's memorial-naming carries the WWII Eesley loss forward into the next generation. The two Lyle Eesleys — the uncle who died in Japanese captivity and the nephew named for him — sit alongside the Charles Leonard-side family's broader wartime memorial pattern that runs through Stella Sunn's arrival in Bexley shortly after the elder Lyle's death (per Roberta Burnes's July 2019 family-email reconstruction).
