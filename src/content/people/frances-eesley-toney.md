Frances Eesley was born in **1901**, the only child of [William Eesley](/family/william-eesley-college-corner/) (Chuck's great-great-uncle, miller at College Corner, Ohio) and [Clara Peel](/family/clara-peel-eesley/) (of Topeka, Kansas). She married **H. Toney**, a farmer near College Corner, and they had one son, **William E. Toney** &mdash; named for his maternal grandfather William.
