Verona B. Sheppard married [Wesley Fleming](/family/wesley-fleming/), and together they raised [Sadye Irene Fleming](/family/sadye-fleming-wildermuth/) and her seven siblings in **Waverly, West Virginia**. Through Verona, the **Sheppard** surname enters the maternal line of Chuck's family — one of several large 19th-century American families the Wildermuth-side ancestry married into.

The Fleming-Sheppard branch is named in the [Dale Eesley FamilySearch tree document](/docs/dale-eesley-familysearch-tree/) as the next-largest maternal connection after the Wildermuths themselves. Sadye's 1976 obituary lists two surviving Fleming sisters — **Mrs. La Verna Raison** of Newark, Ohio, and **Mrs. Etha Becker** of Columbus, Ohio — and notes three brothers and two sisters predeceased Sadye, so Wesley and Verona's household held at least eight children.

This page is a scaffold; dates, biography, and the Sheppard line behind Verona are open work.
