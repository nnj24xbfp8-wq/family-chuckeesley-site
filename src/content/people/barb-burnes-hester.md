Barbara "Barb" Lynn Burnes is the **oldest of [Helen Louise (Eesley) Burnes](/family/helen-burnes/) and Edwin William Burnes's six daughters**, and Chuck's first cousin once removed on the paternal Eesley side. She married **Phillip "Phil" Hester** and took his surname.

## Why she matters to this archive

Barb made **photocopies of all of Grandpa Charles Leonard Eesley's 1940s letters** — the collection Roberta keeps in original form. The photocopy set is the form in which the letters are most likely to leave the immediate family-keeper layer for a reading or transcription pass: the originals can stay with Roberta in Worthington while a working copy circulates for any deciphering work. This is the path Roberta named in her June 2026 correspondence with Chuck: she planned to ask Barb about access to Barb's copies rather than make a fresh set of copies from the brittle originals.

The [1940s Charles Leonard letter collection](/docs/charles-leonard-letter-collection/) page on this archive — which currently describes the collection as held, not yet read, not yet digitized — would be the next layer of documents to land here if Barb's photocopy set ever opens up to a transcription pass.

Details otherwise withheld &mdash; living.
