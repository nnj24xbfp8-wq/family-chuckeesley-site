**William "Bill" Davis Sr.** was the **youngest** of Homer Edward Davis and Bessie Marie ("Marie") Hill Davis's four children &mdash; born **20 August 1941**, more than **fifteen years after** his sister Dorothy (born 1925) and almost **seventeen years after** his eldest sister Mary Louise (1924). His older siblings were:

- Mary Louise Davis Murdock (b. 17 January 1924) &mdash; married name **Murdock** per Betty's 2015 obituary.
- [Dorothy Marie Davis Wildermuth](/family/dorothy-davis-wildermuth/) (b. 24 February 1925) &mdash; **Chuck's grandmother**
- [Betty Jean Davis Wolfe](/family/betty-davis-wolfe/) (b. 16 March 1926) &mdash; **Chuck's great-aunt**
- William Homer Davis Sr. (b. 20 August 1941)

**A note on his middle name.** This page was first built from the **[c. 1992 Davis-Angel family portrait](/archive/davis-angel-family-portrait-1992/)** caption, which identifies him as *"William Harvey Davis, Sr."* &mdash; the source of the **"Harvey"** middle name in the URL slug and the JR-and-SR pair. But his sister Betty Wolfe's **[2015 obituary](/docs/betty-jean-wolfe-obituary-2015/)**, written by her daughters Norma, Victoria, and Peggy, identifies him as **"William Homer Davis"** &mdash; with the middle name **Homer** carrying his father Homer Edward Davis's name down to the youngest son. **The obituary is more recent family writing and a more deliberate genealogical naming.** This archive treats *"William Homer Davis"* as the authoritative version and notes the *"William Harvey Davis, Sr."* label on the 1992 portrait as either a transcription error or an alternate-middle-name usage. The 1992 portrait caption also lists his son as *"William Harvey Davis, Jr."* &mdash; the JR-and-SR pair would have meant *whatever middle name Bill Sr. went by*, his son shared. The current URL slug preserves the "harvey" form for stability but the obituary form is preferred in narrative.

He is the **second-youngest sibling figure on the maternal Wildermuth-Davis side of Chuck's family** &mdash; substantially younger than his sisters, born during the war year his oldest sisters were already in their late teens. His mother Bessie Marie Hill Davis would die in 1950 when Bill was only nine; his father Homer would live to 1982.

## The Davis-Angel marriage

Bill Sr. married **Ida Rowena (Roberts) Davis**, and they had at least one son: **William Harvey Davis Jr.**, who in turn married **[Rebecca "Becky" Angel Davis](/family/rebecca-angel-davis/)** &mdash; the daughter of **[Faye Angel](/family/faye-angel/)** (Chuck Eesley's godmother) and **[Ted Angel](/family/ted-angel/)**. **That marriage is the structural bridge connecting the Angel and Davis-Wildermuth lines** in this archive.

## In the 1992 portrait

He is **back row right** of the **[c. 1992 Davis-Angel family portrait](/archive/davis-angel-family-portrait-1992/)** &mdash; identified in the typed caption as *"William Harvey Davis, Sr."* In his early fifties at the time of the portrait.

He is **Chuck's great-uncle** on the maternal Wildermuth-Davis side &mdash; the youngest brother of Chuck's grandmother Dorothy. **He resides in Carmel, Indiana** per his sister Betty's 2015 obituary &mdash; an Indianapolis suburb that, paired with his daughter-in-law Becky Angel Davis's residence in Fishers, Indiana, places the younger Davis-Angel generation in a tight Indianapolis-area cluster.
