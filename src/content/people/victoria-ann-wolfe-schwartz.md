**Victoria Ann (Wolfe) Schwartz** is the **eldest** of the three daughters of **[Betty Jean (Davis) Wolfe](/family/betty-davis/)** and **[Norman Graham Wolfe](/family/norman-graham-wolfe/)**, the long-time hosts of [108 Hillcrest Drive in Marietta](/family/betty-davis/) where Chuck and his parents spent so many holiday dinners. She is **Chuck's mother [Terrie Lee Wildermuth](/family/terrie-lee-eesley/)'s first cousin** on the maternal Davis side, and was one of the cousins who would have been at the same Hillcrest table as Chuck across the years.

She married **Geoffrey Schwartz** and was living in **Topton, North Carolina** at the time of both her parents' 2015 deaths. Topton is in **Macon County in the western North Carolina mountains** &mdash; a long way from Marietta but the kind of small-town America the Wolfes' values pointed at.

## Her children

- **Geoffrey B. Schwartz II** &mdash; named for his father. **Predeceased his grandparents Norm and Betty in 2009**, a loss that both Wolfe obituaries name explicitly. The grandson's early death is the one shadow on the long-marriage family arc Norm's obituary describes.
- **Emily Marie Schwartz Crouch** &mdash; Vicki's daughter, married Zachary Crouch, lived in **Louisville, Kentucky** at the time of her grandparents' deaths. Mother of **Charlotte Victoria Crouch** (b. c. 2012–2013) &mdash; Charlotte's middle name **Victoria** carries her grandmother Vicki's first name down to the next generation.

The line therefore runs: **Vicki &rarr; Emily Marie &rarr; Charlotte Victoria** &mdash; three generations of Davis-Wolfe women whose middle names hand the family forward.
