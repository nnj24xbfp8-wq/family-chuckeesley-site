**Norman Graham Wolfe** was Chuck's **great-uncle by marriage** on the maternal Wildermuth-Davis side &mdash; the husband of **[Betty Jean Davis Wolfe](/family/betty-davis-wolfe/)**, who was [Dorothy Marie Davis Wildermuth](/family/dorothy-davis-wildermuth/)'s younger sister.

## The GEDCOM record

- Born **15 July 1926 in Marietta, Washington County, Ohio** &mdash; Robert Earl Wildermuth's hometown, two years after Robert Earl himself was born there in 1924. Norm and Robert Earl were near-contemporaries from the same small Ohio River town.
- Married **Betty Jean Davis on 23 December 1945 in Washington County, Ohio** &mdash; just over four months after V-J Day, in the wave of post-war Marietta-area weddings. Both were nineteen. Per [Dorothy Davis Wildermuth's page](/family/dorothy-davis-wildermuth/), her own marriage to Robert Earl on Easter Sunday **20 April 1946** came less than four months after Betty and Norm's wedding &mdash; **the two Davis sisters got married within months of each other** in the same Marietta community.
- Died **9 January 2015 in Largo, Pinellas County, Florida**, age 88 &mdash; presumably after years of Florida retirement, in the Tampa Bay area.
- Buried at **East Lawn Memorial Cemetery, Reno, Washington County, Ohio** &mdash; the same Marietta-area cemetery where Earl Adam Wildermuth is buried.

## 2015 — a heavy year for the family

**Three deaths in 2015 closed the older generation of Chuck's family within months of each other:** Norm Wolfe on 9 January 2015, his wife Betty Wolfe on 18 July 2015 (six months and nine days later), and Chuck's father [Charlie Eesley](/family/charles-eesley/) on 26 November 2015. **All three within eleven months.** The cluster is among the heavier patches of family loss in the archive.
