Details otherwise withheld &mdash; living. Cheryl Cook is the educator and friend-of-the-family who handled the [Harmar Elementary tribute to Mrs. Bain](/docs/mrs-bain-and-the-butterfly-effect/) and shared the text and surrounding context with Chuck during the planning of Terrie's memorial service in June 2017.

The closing image she provided &mdash; the student choir singing "I Believe" (a song Terrie used to listen to through the wall as the students practiced it across the hall), and the four staff members who worked most closely with Terrie releasing balloons as the song ended &mdash; is part of how this archive remembers Terrie's last year of teaching.
