Robert "Bob" Marion Seyler Sr. was the son of [Mae Clara (Wildermuth) Seyler](/family/mae-clara-wildermuth/) and her husband Seyler — born **19 April 1910**, younger brother of [Ruth Mae (Seyler) McIntyre](/family/ruth-mae-seyler-mcintyre/).

He appears at about age 3 in the [c. 1913 photograph of his mother's house on Front Street, Marietta](/archive/wildermuth-sisters-children-front-street-1913/), in a pale belted overcoat and small boots — the youngest standing figure in the frame and the youngest male cousin of the assembled group.

The "Sr." in his name indicates that he later had a son who carried the Robert Marion Seyler name forward to a Jr. generation. His marriage, that son, and the rest of his life are open research. Robert is Chuck Eesley's first cousin twice removed on the paternal Wildermuth side.

> *Sources: [c. 1913 Wildermuth-sisters' children portrait on Front Street](/archive/wildermuth-sisters-children-front-street-1913/) family-labeled print, transmitted by Chuck Eesley June 2026.*
