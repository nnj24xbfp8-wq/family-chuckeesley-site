**Co-pilot** of Robert Earl Wildermuth's B-24, 400th Squadron, 90th Bomb Group ("Jolly Rogers"), Pacific theater 1944–45. From **Hazelton, Pennsylvania** — the anthracite-coal town in northeastern Pennsylvania. Worked alongside pilot [Lt. Maxwell Van Valen](/family/maxwell-van-valen/) in the crew's command pair.

He is named in Robert Earl's [1989 memoir](/docs/robert-earl-wildermuth-memoir/) and the [combat log *The Big One*](/docs/the-big-one/). His pre-war Pennsylvania life and post-war return are open research.

> *Sources: [Robert Earl Wildermuth's 1989 memoir](/docs/robert-earl-wildermuth-memoir/); [The Big One](/docs/the-big-one/).*
