Mary Davis was the **eldest of the three Davis sisters** — older sister of [Dorothy Marie Davis Wildermuth](/family/dorothy-davis-wildermuth/) (Chuck's maternal grandmother) and **[Betty Davis](/family/betty-davis/)**. She was the "one other child of this mother now living" noted on Dorothy's 1925 birth certificate, then approximately two years old.

Daughter of [Homer Edward Davis](/family/homer-davis/) and [Bessie Marie (Hill) Davis](/family/bessie-hill-davis/), born in Ohio around **1923**. The three Davis sisters appear together at ages 5/4/3 and 6/5/4 in two photographs reproduced in [Robert Earl Wildermuth's 1990 Wildermuth/Fleming Heritage](/docs/wildermuth-fleming-heritage-1990/) — Mary on the left in both frames.

She lived through the **Unionville, Ohio house fire** with Dorothy and Betty; the sisters were given a small shed that escaped the fire while their parents took the one-room garage through the cold winter that followed. Mary and Dorothy started school together at **Unionville Grade School** and later transferred to **Marietta High School**.
