Joseph Landai was born **13 August 1715**. On 2 April 1744 he married [Maria Dorothea Wild](/family/maria-dorothea-wild/) of Eschach in the small Württemberg town of Ristenau. The couple settled in Röhrach where Joseph worked as a *day laborer* (Tagelöhner) &mdash; the laboring class one notch below the village farmers his daughter would later marry into.

He died **13 October 1787** in Röhrach, age seventy-two &mdash; just four months after his wife Maria Dorothea (d. 27 June 1787). The couple-of-the-1780s pattern is the kind of detail Württemberg parish records preserve at length.

Robert Earl's [1989 "Little History"](/docs/robert-earl-wildermuth-little-histories/):

> *Joseph Landai was the father of Anna Catherina Landai and a fourth great-grandfather. He was born on the 13th of August 1715. On the 2nd of April 1744, in Ristenau, he married Maria Dorothea Wild. Joseph was a day laborer in Röhrach and he died 13 October 1787. Age 72.*

His daughter [Anna Catherina Landai](/family/anna-catherina-landai/) carries the line forward to the Schmidgall marriage of 1785.

> *Source: [Robert Earl Wildermuth's "Little Histories"](/docs/robert-earl-wildermuth-little-histories/).*
