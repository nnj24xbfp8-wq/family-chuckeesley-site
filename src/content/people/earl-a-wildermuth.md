Earl A. Wildermuth married **[Sadye Irene Fleming](/family/sadye-fleming-wildermuth/)** on **31 December 1920** in Marietta, Ohio. Together they raised five children:

- **[Robert Earl Wildermuth](/family/robert-earl-wildermuth/)** &mdash; Chuck's grandfather, the B-24 navigator and 1948 Stanford BA.
- **Carl E. Wildermuth** &mdash; of Marietta.
- **Ruth I. (Wildermuth) Ridenour** &mdash; of Newark, Ohio.
- **Betty J. (Wildermuth) Haddox** &mdash; of Newark, Ohio.
- **Norma J. (Wildermuth) Gault** &mdash; of Hebron, Ohio.

He outlived Sadye, who died at 75 c. 1976 at Marietta Memorial Hospital. Their household at that point was at **320 Harmar Street** in Marietta.

The existing scaffolding for [Robert Earl's page](/family/robert-earl-wildermuth/) names **Johann Michael Wildermuth** as the family patriarch &mdash; the immigrant shoemaker who set up shop in Marietta in the 1840s &mdash; and characterizes him as Robert Earl's *grandfather*. If that genealogy is correct, then Earl A. is Johann Michael's son. The exact line between Johann Michael and Earl A. is open work for the archive.

> *Source: Sadye I. (Mrs. Earl) Wildermuth obituary, Marietta Times, c. 1976; family collection. Earl's own obituary, if it exists in the family's keeping, would fill the dates on this page.*
