Anna Catherina Landai was born **11 October 1748** in Röhrach, the same small Württemberg village near Aspach where she would later marry [Georg Wilhelm Schmidgall](/family/georg-wilhelm-schmidgall/) (24 April 1785, in nearby Rietenau). Her parents were [Joseph Landai](/family/joseph-landai/), a Röhrach day laborer, and [Maria Dorothea Wild](/family/maria-dorothea-wild/) of Eschach. She died **26 February 1798**, age fifty.

Robert Earl's [1989 "Little History"](/docs/robert-earl-wildermuth-little-histories/) records her as the line connector to the deeper Landai-Wild generation:

> *Anna Catherina was the daughter of Joseph Landai and Maria Dorothea Wild. She was born in Röhrach on the 11th of October 1748 and died 26 February 1798. Age fifty.*

Her daughter [Maria Margaretha Schmidgall](/family/maria-margaretha-schmidgall/) carries the line forward to the Wildermuth marriage of 1842.

Note on the surname: Robert Earl spelled the Landai/Lundai surname inconsistently across his two sketches &mdash; *Lundai* on p. 51 (her parents), *Landai* on p. 54 (her father Joseph). The actual archival spelling is likely a Württemberg variant of *Landauer* or *Landau,* but the family-record spelling Robert Earl used was *Landai.*

> *Source: [Robert Earl Wildermuth's "Little Histories"](/docs/robert-earl-wildermuth-little-histories/).*
