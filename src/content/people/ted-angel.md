**Theodore Ewing "Ted" Angel** was the **husband of [Fay Elsie Heter Angel](/family/faye-angel/)**, Chuck Eesley's godmother &mdash; in a **53-year marriage** that began when the two of them moved to Marietta together in 1939. Together they ran **Angel's Mat and Frame Shop** out of their Marietta home for twelve years &mdash; **the basement filled with thousands of picture frames**, stacked and sorted and waiting for the customer orders that came in over decades.

A small-town family business at this scale &mdash; not a chain, not a factory, but the kind of skilled-trades home-business that operated out of a basement and a workshop &mdash; was a staple of Marietta-area economic life from the 1960s through the 2000s. The Angels' frame business sat in that pattern. **Ted was the maker side**, Faye was the host-and-keeper side; the basement of the house was the workshop.

The Angel home was one of the two **sick-day caregiver households** for young Chuck Eesley &mdash; alongside the Wolfes at [108 Hillcrest Drive](/family/betty-davis-wolfe/). When Chuck couldn't go to school, he was often at Ted and Faye's house, and the picture-framing operation went on around him.

## The Davis-family connection

Ted and Fay's daughter **[Rebecca "Becky" Lynne (Angel) Davis](/family/rebecca-angel-davis/)** married **[William Harvey Davis Sr.](/family/william-harvey-davis-sr/)** &mdash; Chuck's great-uncle, the youngest brother of Chuck's grandmother Dorothy &mdash; on **16 August 1963 in Washington Township, Monroe County, Ohio**. **That marriage made Ted and Fay the parents-in-law of Chuck's maternal great-uncle.** The [c. 1992 Davis-Angel family portrait](/archive/davis-angel-family-portrait-1992/) is the documentary record of that connection, with Ted and Fay, Becky and Bill Sr., and their adult children all together. *The portrait is also likely one of Ted's last photographs* &mdash; per Fay's 2011 obituary, the marriage ran 53 years and Ted predeceased her, placing his death c. **1991–1992**.

## Ted and Fay's other child

The 2011 obituary also names a second child: **David L. Angel of North Lake Tahoe, California** &mdash; Ted and Fay's son, Becky's brother. Ted was preceded in death (per Fay's obituary) by his grandson **Dr. David Angel Jr. M.D.**, presumably David L.'s son, who died as a young physician.
