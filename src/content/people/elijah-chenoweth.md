Elijah Chenoweth is the **father of [Joseph Hill Chenoweth](/family/joseph-hill-chenoweth/)** — and one of the deepest documented ancestors on the Chenoweth side of this archive. He lived in central Ohio in the early-to-mid nineteenth century: his son Joseph Hill was born **30 December 1832 in Pleasant Township, Franklin County, Ohio**, which places Elijah's adult life in that same Franklin-County orbit south of Columbus.

## What this archive knows so far

Elijah is named in the **1860s handwritten Chenoweth family register** that [Roberta Burnes](/family/roberta-burnes/) inherited through the Burnes-Eesley line. That register is the manuscript source that anchors the **[Chenoweth Family Association](https://www.chenowethsite.org/)** online genealogy — the comprehensive surname network for Chenoweth descendants in America. A photograph of Elijah is also held in the same family album in Roberta's keeping; it has not yet been digitized into this archive.

The line that runs through him: **Elijah Chenoweth &rarr; [Joseph Hill Chenoweth](/family/joseph-hill-chenoweth/) &rarr; [Lillie Dale Chenoweth Eesley](/family/lillie-dale-chenoweth/) &rarr; [Wilbur "Will" Eesley](/family/wilbur-eesley/) &rarr; [Charles McMaster Eesley](/family/charles-eesley/) &rarr; Chuck.** Four generations back, four generations forward.

His birth and death dates, spouse, and remaining biographical detail are queued for the next pass against the Roberta-held register and the Chenoweth Family Association's structured records.

> *Sources: [Roberta Burnes](/family/roberta-burnes/), June 2026, on the contents of the Chenoweth family album; [Chenoweth Family Association](https://www.chenowethsite.org/) genealogical site.*
