Howard Edward Wickens &mdash; the groom in the 25 September 1901 wedding photo &mdash; was a Salem Township, Washington County, Ohio man who married into Chuck's maternal Way line through his short marriage to [Belsora "Belle" Way](/family/belsora-way-wickens/).

He was born **19 May 1880 at Salem Township, Washington County, Ohio**. On **25 September 1901**, age 21, he married Belle Way at Noble County, Ohio. The marriage lasted only ten and a half years &mdash; Belle died at 31 in Licking County on 13 April 1912.

Howard outlived her by **fifty-two years**, eventually relocating to Oklahoma. He died **21 January 1964 at Enid City, Garfield County, Oklahoma**, age 83, and was buried at **Enid Cemetery, Oklahoma** &mdash; far from Belle's burial at Crooked Tree, Noble County, Ohio.

Page is a stub. Whether Howard remarried in Oklahoma, and what brought him to Garfield County, are open research items.
