Yaofu was the third of [DeChen Shang](/family/dechen-shang/) and [Chune Tan](/family/chune-tan/)'s four children, brother of [Yaozhen](/family/yaozhen-shang/) (Lijie's grandmother), [Yaoxiang](/family/yaoxiang-shang/), and [Yaoli](/family/yaoli-shang/). He married **[Yunhua Li](/family/yunhua-li/)** &mdash; sister of [Zhongchu Li](/family/zhongchu-li/) (Lijie's grandfather), and so Lijie's great-aunt on the Li side.

That marriage means the **Shang and Li families intermarried in two places at this generation**: Lijie's grandparents (Zhongchu Li + Yaozhen Shang) and Lijie's great-aunt/great-uncle (Yunhua Li + Yaofu Shang). Two Li siblings married two Shang siblings &mdash; a notable family pattern at this layer.

He died in Qingdao in August 2014, age 89.
