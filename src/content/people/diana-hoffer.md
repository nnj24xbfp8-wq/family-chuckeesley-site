**Diana Hoffer** is named in [Charlie Eesley](/family/charles-eesley/)'s published obituary and on his page on this site as his second wife &mdash; the two were married in 1999 and remained married for sixteen years until Charlie's death on 26 November 2015 in Marietta. The marriage covers the second half of Chuck's adolescence, his Duke and MIT years, his joining the Stanford faculty, and his own marriage to Lijie &mdash; the late stretch of Charlie's life in which much of the archive's existing narrative now sits.

Details withheld &mdash; living. The relevant biographical layer carried elsewhere in this archive is on [Charlie's page](/family/charles-eesley/) and in [Chuck's eulogy for his father](/docs/eulogy-charles-mcmaster-eesley/), where Charlie's late life is described from the son's vantage rather than from Diana's.

> *Source: published obituary of Charles McMaster Eesley (Cawley & Peoples Funeral Home, December 2015).*
