Zhou Maoli is the deepest documented Zhou-side ancestor in this archive. He died in **Jiaozhou in 1935** — twelve years before the founding of the People's Republic, in the same city-region where his son Zhaoxiang would later be born in 1930 and on the same land the family traces its origin to. His birth date is not yet recorded.

He married [Huancai Pang](/family/huancai-pang/) and they had three children: [Zhaoxiang Zhou](/family/zhaoxiang-zhou/) (Lijie's grandfather, 1930–2022), [Zhaozheng Zhou](/family/zhaozheng-zhou/) (b. 1938), and [Xiuzhen Zhou](/family/xiuzhen-zhou/). His own parents — [Huiting Zhou](/family/huiting-zhou/) and [Leng Zhou](/family/leng-zhou/) — push the documented line one further generation back.
