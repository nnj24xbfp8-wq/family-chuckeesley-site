Maria Dorothea Wild was born **21 January 1714 in Eschach** &mdash; a small Württemberg village (Robert Earl's memoir prints it as *Eschuch,* almost certainly meaning Eschach). She married [Joseph Landai](/family/joseph-landai/) of Röhrach on 2 April 1744 in Ristenau and moved into his Röhrach household.

She died **27 June 1787 in Röhrach**, age sixty-three. Her husband followed her in death four months later (13 October 1787). Their daughter [Anna Catherina Landai](/family/anna-catherina-landai/) (b. 1748) is the line connector forward to the Schmidgalls and the Wildermuths.

Robert Earl's [1989 "Little History"](/docs/robert-earl-wildermuth-little-histories/):

> *Maria was born in Eschach on 21 January 1714. She died in Röhrach on 27 June 1787. Age sixty-three.*

The Wild surname carries no further in Robert Earl's record; she is the line-end on this side.

> *Source: [Robert Earl Wildermuth's "Little Histories"](/docs/robert-earl-wildermuth-little-histories/); GEDCOM &mdash; Dale Eesley / FamilySearch, sync 1 June 2026.*
