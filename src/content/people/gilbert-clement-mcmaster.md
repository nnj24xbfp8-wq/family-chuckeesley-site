Gilbert Clement McMaster was born in **1833** and died in **1906**, age 73. He was photographed by the **Richards** studio of Pittsburg, Pennsylvania, in the mid-1850s or early 1860s &mdash; the cabinet card, pasted onto dark card stock, shows him as a serious young man with what Maggie Eesley's catalogue describes as a "walrus mustache."

He married [Margaret Thompson McMaster](/family/margaret-thompson-mcmaster/) (1835&ndash;1910), and together they had at least two sons: **Erasmus Darwin McMaster** and **[Robert Thompson McMaster](/family/robert-thompson-mcmaster/)** &mdash; twins born 1866. Erasmus died young of a weakened heart; Robert lived with the same condition until age 55.

He is Maggie Eesley's great-grandfather, which makes him **Chuck's great-great-grandfather** along the line that runs through Robert Thompson McMaster &rarr; [Peggy McMaster Eesley](/family/margaret-mcmaster-eesley/) &rarr; [Charles McMaster Eesley](/family/charles-eesley/) &rarr; Chuck.

> *Source: Maggie Eesley, *Four Generations of the Eesley Family* (PowerPoint archive); deck ID `GCM001 en`; cabinet card by Richards, Pittsburg, PA.*
