Johann Georg Werner is the deepest documented Werner ancestor in Robert Earl Wildermuth's [1989 "Little Histories"](/docs/robert-earl-wildermuth-little-histories/) sketches. His daughter *Anna Maria Werner* (b. 9 June 1739 in [Großaspach](/places/grossaspach/)) is the line connector forward to the rest of the Wildermuth-side ancestry.

He married [Barbara Phillipp](/family/barbara-phillipp/) on **11 February 1738**. Barbara died young, 26 March 1742, age thirty-one, after only four years of marriage. Johann Georg's own birth and death dates are not in the record.

Robert Earl's full sketch is two sentences:

> *Johann Georg Werner, another of our fifth great-grandfathers and the father of Anna Maria Werner, married Barbara Phillipp on the 11th of February 1738. His birth and death dates are unknown.*

> *Source: [Robert Earl Wildermuth's "Little Histories"](/docs/robert-earl-wildermuth-little-histories/); GEDCOM &mdash; Dale Eesley / FamilySearch, sync 1 June 2026.*
