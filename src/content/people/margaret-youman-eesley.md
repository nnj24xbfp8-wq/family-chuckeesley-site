**Margaret Adeline "Peggy" (Youman) Eesley** was the **wife of [Donald Stuart "Don" Eesley](/family/don-eesley/)** — Charles Leonard and Lillie Dale Chenoweth Eesley's third son. She is **"Aunt Peggy Yeoman"** in the family-memory shorthand — distinguished from Chuck's grandmother **Peggy McMaster Eesley** (Will's wife) by the maiden-name suffix.

## Two Peggys in one family

The Eesley generation of Chuck's grandparents had **two Peggy Eesleys** at the same time:

1. **[Peggy McMaster Eesley](/family/margaret-mcmaster-eesley/)** — Chuck's grandmother, Will's wife (married 1942), born 1914 Coraopolis PA.
2. **Peggy Youman Eesley** — Don's wife (married 1938), born 1913 in Ohio.

Both were known as "Peggy" in the family. Both were Margaret Eesleys by marriage. **The "Yeoman/Youman" maiden-name suffix** that the c. 1970s reunion caption uses to identify her was the everyday-family disambiguation.

## The Don Eesley marriage

She married **Don Eesley** (b. 24 September 1908 Lebanon OH; d. 15 May 1975) on **16 June 1938 in Franklin County, Ohio**. The marriage took place during the Charles Leonard Eesley extended family's Columbus / Bexley years; Don was an Ohio State graduate working his way into the electrical / engineering career he would carry through the rest of his life. He died **15 May 1975**, age 66; she survived him.

## In the reunion portrait

She appears second from the right in the **[c. 1970s Eesley extended family reunion portrait](/archive/eesley-extended-family-reunion-portrait-1970s/)** — identified in the typed caption as *"Aunt Peggy Yeoman Eesley."* In her early sixties in the photograph; her husband Don had recently died (May 1975) so the reunion was likely her first post-widowhood visit to the extended-Eesley family gathering.

She is **Chuck's great-aunt by marriage** on the paternal Eesley side.
