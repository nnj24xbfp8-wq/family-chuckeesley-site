Georg Wilhelm Schmidgall was born **12 May 1758** in Röhrach &mdash; the same small Württemberg village (near Kleinaspach in the Marbach district) where he would farm and raise the children who took the line forward. He married [Anna Catherina Landai](/family/anna-catherina-landai/) in Rietenau on 24 April 1785. His daughter [Maria Margaretha Schmidgall](/family/maria-margaretha-schmidgall/) (b. 1789) is the line connector to the Wildermuth-side &mdash; through her 1842 marriage to [Johann Christian Wildermuth](/family/johann-christian-wildermuth/).

Robert Earl's [1989 "Little History" sketch](/docs/robert-earl-wildermuth-little-histories/) carries only the bare facts:

> *Georg was a farmer in Röhrach. His death date is unknown.*

Beyond which the GEDCOM has nothing yet to add. His father [Jacob Schmidgall](/family/jacob-schmidgall/) of Großhöchberg is the next-deepest Schmidgall ancestor on record.
