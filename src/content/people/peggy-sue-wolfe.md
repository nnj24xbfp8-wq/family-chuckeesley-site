**Peggy Sue Wolfe** is the **youngest** of the three daughters of **[Betty Jean (Davis) Wolfe](/family/betty-davis/)** and **[Norman Graham Wolfe](/family/norman-graham-wolfe/)** &mdash; both Marietta Ohio's long-time host couple of 108 Hillcrest Drive and the cousins of Chuck's mother on the maternal Davis side. She is **Chuck's mother [Terrie Lee Wildermuth](/family/terrie-lee-eesley/)'s first cousin** on the Davis side.

She was living in **St. Petersburg, Pinellas County, Florida** at the time of her parents' 2015 deaths &mdash; the same Tampa-Bay area as her sister [Norma Jean](/family/norma-jean-wolfe-grundset/). **The two Florida-cousin households were the gravitational pull for the elder Wolfes' c. 2013 move to Largo for the final two years of their lives** &mdash; the move documented in both her parents' obituaries: *"Betty and Norm spent the final two years of their lives in Largo, Fla., near their daughters."*

She is named in her parents' obituaries by her birth name &mdash; "Peggy Wolfe" in Norm's, "Peggy Sue Wolfe" in Betty's &mdash; without a spouse or married name. Whether Peggy Sue married or stayed Miss Wolfe by choice is open research; the absence of a spouse in the 2015 obituaries suggests at least that she was not married at that time.

Per Betty's obituary, Peggy was at her mother's bedside in her final days: *"Her daughters and her favorite grand dog, Lily, were by her side during her final days reminiscing, laughing, and talking about the love and strength of family."*

> *Sources: [Marietta Times obituary of Norman Graham Wolfe, 21 February 2015](/docs/norman-graham-wolfe-obituary-2015/); [Marietta Times obituary of Betty Jean (Davis) Wolfe, 16 September 2015](/docs/betty-jean-wolfe-obituary-2015/).*
