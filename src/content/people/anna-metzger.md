Anna Metzger was born in **1650** in Württemberg, Germany — like her husband, in the rebuilding years after the Thirty Years' War. She married **[Johannes Wildermuth](/family/johannes-wildermuth-1650/)** (1650&ndash;1694) and was the mother of **[Johannes Wildermuth](/family/johannes-wildermuth-1682/)** (b. 1682).

She died in **1728**, age **78** — a remarkably long life for the period and the region. She outlived her husband by **34 years**, raising or co-raising the next generation through the years that followed his 1694 death.

She is **Chuck's eight-times-great-grandmother** on the maternal Wildermuth line.
