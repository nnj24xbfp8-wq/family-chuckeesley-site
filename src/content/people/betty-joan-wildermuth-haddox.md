**Betty Joan (Wildermuth) Haddox** was the **youngest** of [Earl Adam Wildermuth](/family/earl-a-wildermuth/) and [Sadye Irene (Fleming) Wildermuth](/family/sadye-fleming-wildermuth/)'s five children &mdash; born **17 July 1935 in Marietta, Washington County, Ohio**, the late child of Earl and Sadye's marriage. **She was thirteen years younger than her eldest sister Ruth Irene and ten and a half years younger than her brother Robert Earl** (Chuck's grandfather), arriving in the same Depression-era household as the Wildermuths' youngest.

Her sibling cohort was:

- **[Ruth Irene Wildermuth Ridenour](/family/ruth-irene-wildermuth-ridenour/)** (b. 29 April 1922)
- **[Robert Earl Wildermuth](/family/robert-earl-wildermuth/)** (b. 1924)
- **[Carl Edward Wildermuth](/family/carl-edward-wildermuth/)** (b. 5 July 1928, blind)
- **[Norma Jean Wildermuth Gault](/family/norma-jean-wildermuth-gault/)** (b. 14 September 1930)
- **Betty Joan Wildermuth Haddox** (b. 17 July 1935) &mdash; this page

She lived through the war years as a child (8 when Robert Earl shipped to the Pacific, 10 when V-J Day came), the postwar years of her brother's Stanford studies and Air Force return, and the long arc through her parents' deaths in 1976 and 1977. **She married a Haddox**; her husband's first name and any children are open research.

She **died in 2018, age 82 or 83** &mdash; **the longest-surviving of the five Earl-Adam-Wildermuth siblings**. Her death came forty-one years after her father Earl Adam's, forty-two years after her mother Sadye's, twenty-nine years after Carl's, eleven years after Norma Jean's, and **a year after her niece Terrie Lee Wildermuth Bain's** May 2017 death &mdash; closing the Earl-Adam-Wildermuth generational line on the paternal Wildermuth side of Chuck's family.
