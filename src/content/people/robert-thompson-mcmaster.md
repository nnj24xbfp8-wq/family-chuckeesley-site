Robert Thompson McMaster was born in **1866** to [Gilbert Clement McMaster](/family/gilbert-clement-mcmaster/) and [Margaret Thompson McMaster](/family/margaret-thompson-mcmaster/), one of twin sons. His twin brother, **[Erasmus Darwin McMaster](/family/erasmus-darwin-mcmaster/)**, died young of a weakened heart. Robert carried the same condition and **lived with it until age 55**, dying in **1919**.

Two photographs of him survive in the family archive &mdash; he appears twice in Maggie Eesley's *Four Generations* deck. He was, per the family memory recorded on his daughter Peggy's page, a **civil engineer**.

He married **[Alice Anderson McMaster](/family/alice-anderson-mcmaster/)** (1871&ndash;1961), daughter of [Reverend Abraham Ramsey Anderson](/family/abraham-ramsey-anderson/) the Presbyterian minister. Their children included:

- **[Donald Anderson "Don" McMaster](/family/donald-anderson-mcmaster/)** (1903&ndash;1967) &mdash; photographer; ran the northeastern U.S. communications power grid in WWII.
- **[Margaret Jane "Peggy" McMaster Eesley](/family/margaret-mcmaster-eesley/)** (1914&ndash;2007) &mdash; Chuck's grandmother, who married [Will Eesley](/family/wilbur-eesley/) in 1942.

He died before either Alice (who lived until 1961) or his daughter Peggy reached middle age. Peggy was only **five years old** when her father died; the rest of her growing up was shaped by Alice, the widowed schoolteacher, alone.

He is **Chuck's great-grandfather** through Peggy &rarr; [Charles McMaster Eesley](/family/charles-eesley/) &rarr; Chuck.

> *Sources: Maggie Eesley, *Four Generations of the Eesley Family* (PowerPoint archive); Peggy McMaster Eesley's obituary (*Marietta Times*, 23 May 2007).*
