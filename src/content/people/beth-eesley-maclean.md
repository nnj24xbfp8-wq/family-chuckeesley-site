Details withheld &mdash; living. Daughter of [Daniel Edburn "Dan" Eesley](/family/daniel-edburn-eesley/) and his first wife Joan Allnutt. Sister of [Kate Eesley Grey](/family/kate-eesley-grey/). Married into the MacLean family.

In **2019** she met **Chuck Eesley** in Palo Alto along with her sister Kate &mdash; the documented in-person reconnection of the two parallel Albert Robert Sr Eesley branches (the Charles Leonard line through Chuck, the R. Albert line through Dan), after more than a century separated by geography. See [Dan's page](/family/daniel-edburn-eesley/) for the meeting in family context.

> *Source: [Daniel Edburn Eesley obituary, 2018](/docs/daniel-edburn-eesley-obituary-2018/).*
