Alfred Eesley appears in the **1899 family group portrait caption** as one of [Albert Robert Eesley Sr](/family/albert-robert-eesley/) and [Jennie Goldie](/family/jeanie-goldie/)'s ten children, but he does **not** appear by that name in [William Eesley's c. 1916 obituary](/docs/william-eesley-obituary-college-corner/), which lists the six miller-sons as *John R., William, Robert, A. R. (Jr.), C. L., and Garfield.*

Three possible readings:

1. **Alfred was the obituary's "Robert."** The obituary may have used a middle name or a separate family name; Alfred Robert Eesley or similar.
2. **Alfred died between 1899 and 1916** and the obituary omitted him from the surviving-children list (though obituaries of the era often included deceased children).
3. **William's obituary made an error or omission**, and Alfred was a separate son not represented in the obituary's roster at all.

The 1899 caption is closer in time to the family's direct memory; the 1916 obituary is one source-removed. Either could be wrong. The reconciliation is queued.

He is **Chuck's great-great-uncle** on the paternal side either way.
