DeChen Shang is the father of the four Shang siblings born in early-20th-century Qingdao — [Yaozhen](/family/yaozhen-shang/) (Lijie's grandmother), [Yaoxiang](/family/yaoxiang-shang/), [Yaofu](/family/yaofu-shang/), and [Yaoli](/family/yaoli-shang/). His birth and death are not yet recorded in this archive.
