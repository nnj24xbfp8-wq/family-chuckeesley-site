**Katie Burnes** is the **daughter of [Helen Louise (Eesley) Burnes](/family/helen-burnes/)** and **Edwin William Burnes**, and **sister of [Roberta Marie Burnes Walker](/family/roberta-burnes/)** — the keeper of the 1940s Charles Leonard Eesley correspondence collection. Helen Burnes had two daughters with Edwin; Katie and Roberta are both visible in the c. 1970s Eesley extended family reunion portrait.

Katie is Chuck's **first cousin once removed** on the paternal Eesley side — daughter of his great-aunt Helen Louise Eesley Burnes.

## In the reunion portrait

She appears at the left side of the **[c. 1970s Eesley extended family reunion portrait](/archive/eesley-extended-family-reunion-portrait-1970s/)** — a young woman seated, **identified in the typed caption** simply as *"Katie Burnes (Helen Burnes' daughter)."* Her exact birth date is not yet documented in this archive; based on her apparent age in the c. 1970s reunion photo, she was plausibly born in the **late 1940s or early 1950s** — after her parents' 3 August 1946 marriage.

She does **not yet appear in Dale Eesley's FamilySearch GEDCOM tree** as a structured entry under Helen and Edwin Burnes's family record. This is the kind of next-generation gap a future research pass against the Burnes-family records of the 1950s onward would close.

> *Source: [c. 1970s Eesley extended family reunion portrait](/archive/eesley-extended-family-reunion-portrait-1970s/) typed caption — the family's primary identification of her.*
