John R. Eesley was the **eldest** of [Albert Robert Eesley Sr](/family/albert-robert-eesley/) and [Jennie Goldie](/family/jeanie-goldie/)'s ten children — born in Ontario, Canada, during the family's early years there (the first two children, J.R. and Anna, were born in Canada before Albert Robert began his Michigan milling years per [William Eesley's c. 1916 obituary](/docs/william-eesley-obituary-college-corner/)).

He **followed his father into milling**, one of the six Eesley brothers who became millers. Like several of his siblings he is identified in the [1899 family group portrait](/family/charles-leonard-eesley/) (caption: *"John (oldest)"*) — the studio photograph of Albert Robert Sr, Jennie Goldie, and all ten of their children together.
