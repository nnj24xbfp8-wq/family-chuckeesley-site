Frances appears only by name in the surviving records — wife of Joseph Eesley, mother of at least four children baptized between 1787 and 1799: William (1787, 19 Aug), Hannah (1790, 23 May), Mary (1794, 25 Dec), and John (1799, Jan 6). Her maiden name is not yet established.
