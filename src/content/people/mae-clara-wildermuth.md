**Mae Clara Wildermuth** was the **eldest** of the nine children of [William Clifford Wildermuth](/family/william-wildermuth/) and [Flora Schlicher Wildermuth](/family/flora-schlicher-wildermuth/). She was the **oldest aunt** of Chuck's grandfather [Robert Earl Wildermuth](/family/robert-earl-wildermuth/) on the paternal Wildermuth side &mdash; one of his **father [Earl Adam](/family/earl-a-wildermuth/)'s elder sisters**.

Born **5 May 1886 in Marietta, Washington County, Ohio**, Mae Clara was the first of the long line of Wildermuth siblings who would emerge from the 1886 Marietta marriage. She lived her **whole life in Marietta** and died there **23 October 1939**, age 53. The household-Marietta arc covers the entire Wildermuth-family expansion her father William Clifford anchored at [413 Harmar Street](/places/413-harmar-street-marietta/) and the surrounding West Side neighborhood.

## In the family record

Mae Clara is named as a sibling on her brother [Earl Adam Wildermuth's page](/family/earl-a-wildermuth/) and in [Robert Earl Wildermuth's 1990 Wildermuth/Fleming Heritage](/docs/wildermuth-fleming-heritage-1990/). Whether she married, had children, or appears in surviving family photographs is open research.

Her sibling cohort (b. 1886 to 1902): Mae Clara (1886), [Charles Daniel](/family/charles-daniel-wildermuth/) (1889), [Margaret Zelma](/family/margaret-zelma-wildermuth/) (1891), [Emma](/family/emma-wildermuth/) (1893), [William Jr.](/family/william-wildermuth-jr/) (1896 &mdash; d. 1897 in infancy), [Earl Adam](/family/earl-a-wildermuth/) and his twin **[Pearl](/family/pearl-wildermuth/)** (both 1899), [George D.](/family/george-d-wildermuth/) (1902), and [Blanche](/family/blanche-wildermuth/) (dates unrecorded, presumed early death). Mae Clara was the eldest of the nine.
