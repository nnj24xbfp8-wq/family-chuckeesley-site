**Rebecca "Becky" Lynne Angel Davis** is **the daughter of [Faye Angel](/family/faye-angel/) (Chuck's godmother) and [Theodore "Ted" Angel](/family/ted-angel/)** &mdash; and the wife of **[William Harvey Davis Sr.](/family/william-harvey-davis-sr/)**, the youngest brother of Chuck's grandmother [Dorothy Davis Wildermuth](/family/dorothy-davis-wildermuth/) and great-aunt [Betty Davis Wolfe](/family/betty-davis-wolfe/).

## The marriage that joined the two families &mdash; 16 August 1963

Becky's marriage to Bill Sr. is the **structural genealogical bridge** between the Angel and Davis-Wildermuth lines:

- **Date and place:** 16 August 1963, Washington Township, Monroe County, Ohio.
- Bill Sr. had just turned 22; Becky was the young daughter of the Marietta-College-Book-Store-and-picture-framing-business Angels.
- **Through the marriage, Faye and Ted Angel became the in-laws of the Davis line.**
- **Becky is Chuck's great-aunt by marriage** &mdash; specifically the wife of his maternal great-uncle Bill Sr.
- **Faye Angel becoming Chuck's godmother in 1979** is the natural godparent choice for someone whose daughter is already married into Chuck's grandmother's brother's branch &mdash; not just close friendship but family-by-marriage.

## Children

Becky and Bill Sr. had **at least one son and one daughter**. Three children identified in the [c. 1992 Davis-Angel family portrait](/archive/davis-angel-family-portrait-1992/) and presumed to be Becky and Bill Sr.'s:

- **William Harvey Davis Jr.** &mdash; their eldest son, named for his father.
- **Homer Edward Davis** &mdash; presumed second son, named for his grandfather Homer Edward Davis Sr.
- **Kimberly Ann Davis** &mdash; presumed daughter, the youngest figure in the front row of the 1992 portrait.

## In the 1992 portrait

Becky is **front row center-right** of the **[c. 1992 Davis-Angel family portrait](/archive/davis-angel-family-portrait-1992/)** &mdash; identified in the typed caption as *"Rebecca 'Becky' Lynne (Angel) Davis,"* seated between her mother Faye Angel and her sister-in-law Betty Wolfe (Bill Sr.'s sister), with her husband Bill Sr. behind her in the back row. The full-maiden-and-married name pattern of the caption is the family's deliberate documentation of the marriage that joined the two families.

## Later life in Indiana

At her mother Faye's death in November 2011, Becky was living in **Fishers, Indiana** &mdash; an Indianapolis suburb. Bill Sr. by 2008 lived in **Gap Mills, West Virginia**; whether they were still together at the time of Faye's death or had separated is open research. Bill Sr. died in 2022 in West Virginia.
