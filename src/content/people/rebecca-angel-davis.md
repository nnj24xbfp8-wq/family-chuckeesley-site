**Rebecca "Becky" Lynne Angel Davis** is **the daughter of [Faye Angel](/family/faye-angel/) (Chuck's godmother) and [Theodore "Ted" Angel](/family/ted-angel/)** &mdash; and the wife of **William Harvey Davis Jr.**, son of William Harvey Davis Sr. (Betty and Dorothy Davis's younger brother).

## The marriage that joined the two families

Becky's marriage to William Harvey Davis Jr. is **the genealogical bridge that joins the Angel and Davis-Wildermuth families** in the archive. Through that marriage:

- **Faye and Ted Angel** become the in-laws of the Davis line.
- **Becky** is Chuck's first cousin once removed by marriage (her husband William Jr. is the son of Chuck's great-uncle Bill Davis Sr.).
- **Faye Angel becoming Chuck's godmother in 1979** is the natural godparent choice for someone whose daughter is married into Chuck's mother's first cousin's branch &mdash; not just close friendship but family-by-marriage.

## In the 1992 portrait

Becky is **front row center** of the **[c. 1992 Davis-Angel family portrait](/archive/davis-angel-family-portrait-1992/)** &mdash; identified in the typed caption as *"Rebecca 'Becky' Lynne (Angel) Davis."* The full-maiden-and-married name pattern of the caption is itself the family's deliberate documentation of the marriage that joined the two families.

Becky's birth and marriage dates, her children, and her own life beyond the marriage are open research.

> *Source: [c. 1992 Davis-Angel family portrait](/archive/davis-angel-family-portrait-1992/) typed caption; transmitted by Chuck Eesley 2026.*
