Details withheld &mdash; living. Sandra Sue is Robert Earl Wildermuth and Dottie Davis's eldest other daughter, sister of Chuck's mother Terrie Lee. She accompanied Robert Earl on the [November 1992 trip to Rielingshausen and Marbach](/places/rielingshausen-town-square/) and his caption on the print of the town fountain identifies her by maiden name and married name: *"Sandra Sue (Wildermuth) Clement in front of the Town Square Fountain."* She also appears in the [1965 family return to Stanford](/places/stanford-university/).

Per her sister Terrie's 2017 obituary she was living in **Atlanta, Georgia** at that point.
