Details withheld &mdash; living. Sandra Sue is Robert Earl Wildermuth and Dottie Davis's daughter, sister of Chuck's mother Terrie Lee. She accompanied Robert Earl on the [November 1992 trip to Rielingshausen and Marbach](/places/rielingshausen-town-square/) and his caption on the print of the town fountain identifies her by maiden name and married name: *"Sandra Sue (Wildermuth) Clement in front of the Town Square Fountain."* She appears again in the [1980s family return to Stanford](/places/stanford-university/).
