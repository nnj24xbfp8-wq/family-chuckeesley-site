Wesley Fleming was the father of [Sadye Irene Fleming](/family/sadye-fleming-wildermuth/), who married Earl A. Wildermuth and became Robert Earl Wildermuth's mother. He and his wife **[Verona B. Sheppard Fleming](/family/verona-sheppard-fleming/)** raised Sadye in **Waverly, West Virginia** — the Pleasants County town on the Ohio River south of Marietta where Sadye was born on 15 July 1901.

The Fleming-and-Sheppard line is one of the two major maternal connections on Chuck's mother's side noted in the [Dale Eesley FamilySearch tree](/docs/dale-eesley-familysearch-tree/) — Sadye's obituary mentions she had **seven siblings** (three brothers and two sisters predeceased her; two sisters — Mrs. La Verna Raison of Newark, OH and Mrs. Etha Becker of Columbus, OH — survived), so Wesley and Verona's household was a large one.
