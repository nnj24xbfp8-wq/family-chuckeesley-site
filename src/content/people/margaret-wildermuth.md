Margaret Wildermuth was one of the **nine children of [William Clifford Wildermuth](/family/william-wildermuth/) and [Flora Schlicher Wildermuth](/family/flora-schlicher-wildermuth/)**, born in Marietta, Ohio. Her name appears in [Earl Adam Wildermuth's 17 January 1972 family-history letter](/docs/letters/earl-adam-to-robert-earl-1972-01-17/) in the birth-order roster:

> *yes all of my brothers and sister were born in Marietta. May, Charlie, William, Margrette, Blanche, Emma, Earl and Pearl and George.*

She sits **fourth** in Earl Adam's recollected order &mdash; between her older brother William and her younger sister Blanche &mdash; which would put her birth roughly in the middle of the 1890s, but the exact year is open research. The *"Margrette"* spelling is Earl Adam's; the formal name was almost certainly Margaret.

Earl Adam provided no further detail about her adult life in the 1972 letter &mdash; no marriage, no residence, no death. Marriage records, census records, and Marietta-area death records would be the natural next-pass sources to fill out her biography. Her sister [Blanche Wildermuth](/family/blanche-wildermuth/), older sister [Mae Clara Wildermuth](/family/mae-clara-wildermuth/), and younger sisters [Emma Wildermuth](/family/emma-wildermuth/) and [Pearl Wildermuth](/family/pearl-wildermuth/) all have their own pages on this site.

> *Source: [Earl Adam Wildermuth's 17 January 1972 letter to Robert Earl Wildermuth](/docs/letters/earl-adam-to-robert-earl-1972-01-17/), p. 2.*
