Helen Louise Eesley was born **January 16, 1924** in Columbus, Ohio — the youngest of Charles Leonard and Lillie Dale Chenoweth Eesley's ten children, born the same year her older sister Jean Goldie Eesley died at twelve. She married **Edwin William Burnes** and lived in Worthington, Ohio.

She and Edwin had at least seven children — a son Brian and six daughters. Four of the six daughters kept the Burnes name through adulthood:

- **Brian Carlson Burnes** (b. March 6, 1948).
- **[Barbara "Barb" Lynn Burnes Hester](/family/barb-burnes-hester/)** (b. September 13, 1950), the oldest of the daughters; married Phillip Hester (took his name). Barb made photocopies of all of Grandpa Charles Leonard's 1940s letters; the photocopied set is the most likely entry point for any future deciphering pass through that collection.
- **[Elizabeth "Liz" Burnes](/family/liz-burnes/)** (b. May 15, 1955); married Donald Faux but kept her own surname.
- **[Cindy Burnes](/family/cindy-burnes/)**; kept her own surname.
- **[Shelley Burnes](/family/shelley-burnes/)**; kept her own surname.
- **[Roberta Marie Burnes](/family/roberta-burnes/)** (b. August 6, 1963); married John Walker but kept her own surname. The keeper of the 1940s Eesley letter collection in its original form and one of the four paternal-side keepers credited on this archive's [homepage](/).
- **[Katherine "Katie" Burnes McKeel](/family/katie-burnes/)** (b. February 15, 1965); married into the McKeel family.

Four of Helen and Edwin's six daughters kept the Burnes surname when they married — Liz, Cindy, Shelley, and Roberta — and two (Barb and Katie) took their husbands' names. The pattern is itself a small piece of mid-twentieth-century-into-modern women's-naming history in this family.

Helen and Mary Bean were the two sisters who survived Will in 1986.

> *Source: Mary Eesley Bean, *[Eesley Family History](/docs/eesley-family-history-1985/)*, p. 8.*
