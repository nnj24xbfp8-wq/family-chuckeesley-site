Jean Goldie Eesley was the sixth child of Charles Leonard and Lillie Dale Chenoweth Eesley. She was named for her grandmother — Jeanie Goldie, the Scottish immigrant matriarch on the paternal side. She lived twelve years.

The 1985 *Eesley Family History* records her with one line: *"IV. Jean Goldie Eesley b. Oct. 28, 1912, Grove City Ohio. d. died 1924, Columbus Ohio."* What she died of is not recorded. She is the predeceased sister Will's 1986 obituary refers to.
