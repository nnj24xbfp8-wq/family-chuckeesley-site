Maggie is the immediate predecessor of this site. Her hyperlinked four-generation photo deck — built around four lines (Eesley / Chenoweth on her father's side, McMaster / Anderson on her mother's side) — uses full archival catalogue metadata for each item: Title, Type, Medium, Measurement, Condition, Creator, Date Created, Place Created, ID Number, Provenance, Description, Inscription, Location/Rights. The `artifacts` schema in this archive maps almost one-to-one onto her fields, so her work imports nearly losslessly.

Without her there would be no portraits in this archive. Details withheld here &mdash; living.
