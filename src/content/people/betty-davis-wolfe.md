**Betty Jean (Davis) Wolfe** was the **younger sister of [Dorothy Marie Davis Wildermuth](/family/dorothy-davis-wildermuth/)** &mdash; Chuck's grandmother &mdash; making her Chuck's **great-aunt** on the maternal Wildermuth-Davis side.

## The Davis sisters

Born **16 March 1926 in McConnelsville, Morgan County, Ohio** &mdash; about thirty miles north of Marietta &mdash; she was the **fourth of five children** of [Homer Edward Davis](/family/homer-davis/) (1900&ndash;1982) and [Bessie Marie Hill Davis](/family/bessie-hill-davis/) (1905&ndash;1950). Her older siblings were Mary Louise (b. 1924) and [Dorothy Marie](/family/dorothy-davis-wildermuth/) (b. 24 February 1925 &mdash; just thirteen months her senior). A younger brother William Harvey was born much later, in 1941.

**Betty and Dorothy were nearly the same age** &mdash; just over a year apart &mdash; and grew up together through the Marietta-area 1930s.

## The post-war marriages

Betty married **[Norman Graham Wolfe](/family/norman-graham-wolfe/)** on **23 December 1945 in Washington County, Ohio**, when both were nineteen &mdash; just over four months after V-J Day. Norm was a Marietta-born hometown boy of the same 1926 birth year.

Four months later, on **Easter Sunday 20 April 1946**, her older sister Dorothy married [Robert Earl Wildermuth](/family/robert-earl-wildermuth/) at the preacher's home in Marietta. **The Davis sisters were both married into the Marietta community within a single five-month span**, in the wave of post-war weddings that closed out the war years across small-town America. Their mother Bessie was alive for both weddings (she would die in 1950).

## The Florida retirement and the close 2015 deaths

Betty and Norm eventually settled in **Largo, Pinellas County, Florida** &mdash; Tampa-Bay area &mdash; for their retirement. Norm died there on **9 January 2015**. Betty followed **six months and nine days later**, on **18 July 2015**, age 89. The closeness of the dates is the kind of long-marriage end the archive sees often.

She was buried alongside Norm at **East Lawn Memorial Cemetery, Reno, Washington County, Ohio** &mdash; back in the Marietta-area cemetery where she had grown up, near where Earl Adam Wildermuth (Robert Earl's father) is buried.

## 2015 — a heavy family year

Betty's death in July 2015 was the **second of three deaths in the immediate family that year**. Norm had died in January 2015; **Chuck's father [Charlie](/family/charles-eesley/) would die on 26 November 2015**, four months and eight days after Betty. Eleven months from Norm's January death to Charlie's November death &mdash; three of Chuck's older-generation family figures gone in a single year.
