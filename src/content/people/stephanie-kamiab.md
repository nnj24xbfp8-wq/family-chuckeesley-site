Stephanie ("Stephie") Kamiab is the daughter of [Aunt Jeanne (Eesley) Kamiab](/family/jeanne-eesley-kamiab/) and Joseph "Joe" Kamiab &mdash; Chuck Eesley's first cousin on the paternal Eesley side. Her parents were married in Marietta in June 1965, the wedding her uncle Charlie names in his [12th-grade autobiography](/docs/charles-eesley-12th-grade-autobiography-1965/) and the family-with-Joe-Kamiab portrait above captures.

She is named in passing in Charlie's late-1970 letter to Terrie from Vietnam &mdash; *"Jeanne writes and says that Stephie already calls you Aunt Terrie"* &mdash; the earliest reference to her by name in this archive. The line places her as a small child whose adoption of *"Aunt Terrie"* for her uncle's then-girlfriend already, before either Charlie and Terrie's marriage or her own first cousin Chuck's 1979 birth, was one of the small family signals that Charlie and Terrie were heading to a wedding.

Other details withheld &mdash; living.
