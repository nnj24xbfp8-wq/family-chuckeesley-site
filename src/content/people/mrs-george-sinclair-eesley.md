The daughter of [Albert Robert Eesley Sr](/family/albert-robert-eesley/) and [Jennie Goldie](/family/jeanie-goldie/) who appears in [William Eesley's c. 1916 obituary](/docs/william-eesley-obituary-college-corner/) only as **"Mrs. George Sinclair, also of [Toronto]"** — the era's convention of identifying married women solely by their husband's name. Her given Eesley first name is **not yet documented** in this archive.

The two candidates from the **1899 family group portrait caption** are *"[Bessie](/family/bessie-eesley/)"* and *"[Jennie](/family/jennie-baxter-eesley/)"* — but Jennie's husband's name is documented as Baxter, leaving Bessie as the most parsimonious match. **If Bessie was her given name and George Sinclair her husband, her full name would have been Bessie (Eesley) Sinclair.** Reconciliation is queued.

She is **Chuck's great-great-aunt** on the paternal side and lived in Toronto alongside her siblings Anna Scoon, Dr. George the physician, and Mrs. Jennie Baxter.
