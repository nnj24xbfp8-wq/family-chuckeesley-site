Sun Yunzhe survived her husband [Zhaoxiang Zhou](/family/zhaoxiang-zhou/) by less than a year &mdash; he died 9 December 2022; she died December 2023, also in Qingdao. They had been married for over half a century.

She is the mother of [Lijie's father Ling Zhou](/family/ling-zhou/) and his three siblings. Birth date and earlier life are not yet recorded in this archive.
