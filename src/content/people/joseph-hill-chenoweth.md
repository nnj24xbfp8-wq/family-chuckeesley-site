Joseph Hill Chenoweth was born **30 December 1832 in Pleasant Township, Franklin County, Ohio** &mdash; the same Pleasant Township just south of Columbus where his daughters Lillie Dale (1877) and Scioto Mafry (1871) would also be born. He married **[Mary O. Timmons](/family/mary-ohio-timmons-chenoweth/) on 25 November 1864** in Franklin County, and together they were the parents of at least five children: [Lillie Dale](/family/lillie-dale-chenoweth/) (the Chenoweth matriarch who married into the Eesley line), [Scioto Mafry](/family/scioto-mafry-chenoweth/) (the medical pioneer of family memory), Howard Glen, Elsie, and Dwight Kennedy.

He died in **June 1910**, age 77, and is buried at **Otterbein Cemetery in Westerville, Franklin County, Ohio** &mdash; the same cemetery his daughter Scioto would be buried at twenty years later.

He is **Chuck's great-great-grandfather** along the path that runs through his daughter Lillie Dale to Will Eesley and onward.

The only image of him in the family archive is a formal portrait dating roughly **1865&ndash;1870**, scanned from a CD held by his great-granddaughter [Roberta Burnes Walker](/family/roberta-burnes/) and emailed by her brother John Walker to Maggie Eesley for the *Four Generations* archive. The scan resolution, in Maggie's note, is "barely good enough to enlarge image" &mdash; he is at the edge of what photographic preservation reaches for this generation.
