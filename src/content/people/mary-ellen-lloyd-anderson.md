Born **1859 in Ontario, Canada**; married [John Wallace Anderson](/family/john-wallace-anderson/) on **21 May 1888 in Emporia, Lyon County, Kansas**; died **1896 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny County, Pennsylvania** at age 37. The Ontario birth, Kansas marriage, and Pittsburgh death triangulate a life that crossed the continent twice in eight years — the Canadian-to-American immigration pattern of the 1880s, followed by the western-to-eastern relocation that brought her back to her husband's Pennsylvania family before her death. Cause of death and burial place are queued.
