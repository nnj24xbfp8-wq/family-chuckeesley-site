**The Will and Peggy Eesley family at the moment Jeanne brought Joe home** &mdash; a formal indoor portrait of **the six-of-them plus Joe Kamiab**, made around **Jeanne and Joe's June 1965 wedding**. The family snapshot of **Will and Peggy with all four of their children and the new son-in-law who would marry their eldest daughter** &mdash; the marriage [Charlie's 12th-grade autobiography](/docs/charles-eesley-12th-grade-autobiography-1965/) names specifically.

## The cast (queued for confirmation)

**Front row (seated):**
- **[Margaret 'Peggy' McMaster Eesley](/family/margaret-mcmaster-eesley/)** &mdash; the mother, in a dark sweater with curly hair, hands folded on a coffee table that holds magazines.
- **[Wilbur 'Will' Eesley](/family/wilbur-eesley/)** &mdash; the father, the Marietta architect, in a dark suit and tie with glasses.

**Back row (on the couch), left to right** &mdash; confirmed by Chuck Eesley June 2026 that **Jeanne sits next to Joe**:

- **[Charles 'Charlie' McMaster Eesley](/family/charles-eesley/)** &mdash; **far left, blonde buzz cut, dark shirt** &mdash; Chuck's father, then about eighteen, between the 1965 high school graduation and Marietta College. The face that matches the c. 1965 [Marietta HS cross country team photo](/archive/marietta-hs-cross-country-team-c-1964/) and the next decade of Vietnam-era letters.
- **[Anne Eesley](/family/anne-eesley/)** &mdash; **second from left, dark wavy hair, plaid jacket** &mdash; the second daughter.
- **[Margaret 'Maggie' Eesley](/family/margaret-maggie-eesley/)** &mdash; **middle, light-haired girl in white sweater** &mdash; the youngest, about thirteen or fourteen.
- **[Jeanne Eesley](/family/jeanne-eesley-kamiab/)** &mdash; **fourth from left, light short hair, dark top, beside Joe** &mdash; the eldest daughter, about to be or just-married to Joe.
- **Joseph 'Joe' Kamiab** &mdash; **far right, dark curly hair, light vest over dark shirt** &mdash; Jeanne's husband, the new addition to the family in this frame.

## Why this date

Charlie's [1965 12th-grade autobiography](/docs/charles-eesley-12th-grade-autobiography-1965/) tells us **Jeanne married Joe Kamiab in June 1965**. The ages of all four children in the frame fit that year almost exactly: Jeanne mid-twenties, Anne about nineteen, Charlie eighteen, Maggie thirteen-to-fourteen &mdash; the same year Charlie graduated Marietta High School. **The portrait was almost certainly made at the wedding or in the surrounding weeks**, in the family's [651 Sixth Street](/places/651-sixth-street-marietta/) house or nearby.

## What this frame captures

**The last family portrait of the original household before it began breaking up into adult lives.** Jeanne marries; Charlie goes to Marietta College and then Vietnam (1969&ndash;71); Anne moves on; Maggie completes the round of college after them. Will continues remodeling 651 Sixth Street and then designs and builds the [Highland Ridge Road](/places/highland-ridge-road-marietta/) house. The six-with-Joe-Kamiab portrait holds **the moment the family was, just before it became its many later configurations**.
