**Zhaoxiang Zhou 周兆祥** seated in front, smiling broadly &mdash; in the gray jacket and dark padded vest of an elderly winter day in Qingdao. Standing behind, left to right: **Chuck Eesley** (brown corduroy jacket, dark turtleneck), **Lijie 周丽婕** (with the wider smile of a daughter-with-her-grandfather moment), **[Ai Hua Zhou 周爱华](/family/ai-hua-zhou/)** &mdash; Lijie's paternal aunt, daughter of Zhaoxiang &mdash; in a striking white-and-blue flowered top, and **[her husband](/family/ai-hua-zhou-husband/)** on the right in a navy v-neck sweater. Behind them, the same vivid folk-art mural of blue and red flowers that anchors the [companion photograph with Lijie's parents](/archive/visit-with-zhaoxiang-zhou-and-family/) &mdash; the two photographs were almost certainly taken on the same visit, with different branches of Zhaoxiang's household each having a frame.

For Ai Hua's page, this is her **first appearance in the archive**, alongside her father and her husband &mdash; a three-generation Chinese family frame anchored by the elder in the middle.

## 中文

**与周兆祥合影 &mdash; 周爱华夫妇、Lijie与Chuck**

前排坐者：**周兆祥**笑容满面 &mdash; 身穿青岛冬日老人常见的灰色夹克与深色棉背心。后排立者由左至右：**Chuck Eesley**（棕色灯芯绒夹克、深色高领毛衣）、**周丽婕**（与祖父合影时格外开怀的笑容）、**[周爱华](/family/ai-hua-zhou/)** &mdash; Lijie的姑母，周兆祥的女儿 &mdash; 身着醒目的白底蓝花上衣、以及**[周爱华之夫](/family/ai-hua-zhou-husband/)** 着海军蓝V领毛衣。背景墙上是与[Lijie父母合影](/archive/visit-with-zhaoxiang-zhou-and-family/)同一面蓝、红花卉民间艺术墙画 &mdash; 两张照片极有可能为同一次探访所摄，周兆祥的两支子女家庭各得一张。

对周爱华本人来说，这是她在本档案中**首次现身** &mdash; 与父亲、丈夫同框，三代同堂之中国家庭画面。
