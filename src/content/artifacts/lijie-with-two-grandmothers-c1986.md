Lijie as a toddler — likely three or four years old, so the photograph dates to about 1986–87 — seated at the family's round wooden dining table between **both of her grandmothers**: **Yaozhen Shang 商耀珍** (maternal, on her right by the photographer's frame, in dark Mao-style jacket and round glasses) and **Yunzhe Sun 孙云哲** (paternal, on her left, in dark coat). The two elders are smiling. The table is laid out with **Chinese New Year-style dishes** — sliced sausage, marinated cold cuts, a colorful pickled radish salad, stir-fried greens — the chopsticks resting on small porcelain rests, three drinking cups arranged at the place settings. A daily calendar hangs upper-right; the interior is the unmistakable warm domestic register of 1980s urban Chinese home life: blue-painted wood-frame windows, painted plaster walls, the carved wooden chest of drawers in the corner.

This is the single most consequential photograph of Lijie's early childhood now in the archive. Both grandmothers are alive — Yaozhen and Yunzhe both. **Zhongchu Li** (the maternal grandfather) had died in 1982; **Zhaoxiang Zhou** (the paternal grandfather) was still alive in Jiaozhou but is not in this frame. The two surviving grandmothers and the small granddaughter at the same table is what passes for the most universal Chinese family-table photograph — the year's reunion meal, with the eldest generation and the youngest in the same room.

Yaozhen would live another twenty-six years; Yunzhe another thirty-six. Lijie would marry Chuck in 2015 — Yaozhen alive to know about it; Yunzhe alive at her wedding day. The arc of how long the two women in this 1986 photograph would live, after this small frame, is one of the structural facts of Lijie's biography.

## 中文

**Lijie与两位祖母合影，青岛，约1986–87年**

Lijie约三四岁时与**外祖母 [商耀珍](/family/yaozhen-shang/)**（左侧戴眼镜者）和**祖母 [孙云哲](/family/yunzhe-sun/)**（右侧着深色外套者）同坐圆桌前 &mdash; 桌上摆满了中式过年节日菜肴：拼盘冷切、酱肉、凉拌萝卜、青菜、米饭；筷架、瓷杯均备齐。墙上挂日历，房间陈设为典型1980年代青岛家庭室内：蓝漆木窗、抹灰墙、雕花木箱。

这是本档案中Lijie幼年最具意义的照片。当时**李忠初**（外祖父）已于1982年辞世；**周兆祥**（祖父）尚在世，但不在此画面中。两位健在的祖母与幼小的外孙女同桌 &mdash; 即中国家庭最常见、最普遍的"团圆饭"画面。

商耀珍后又活了二十六年，孙云哲又活了三十六年。Lijie与Chuck于2015年结婚 &mdash; 商耀珍来得及知道，孙云哲也来得及在结婚那年仍在世。这张1986年小框中的两位长者，在此之后究竟还能陪伴Lijie多久，本身已是Lijie人生的一个结构性事实。
