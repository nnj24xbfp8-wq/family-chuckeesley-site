Terrie at the information desk, a clock on the wall, a tiger artwork above her, ranked shelves of reference books behind her. She is leaning into the desk, hands folded, smiling directly at the camera.

This is the librarian half of *"my books and students"* in her [poem](/docs/please-dont-cry-for-me-terrie-bain/), and the *"former children's librarian"* in Chuck's [memorial speech](/docs/memorial-speech-terrie-lee-bain/). Most likely from her years at the **Marietta Public Library** as children's librarian &mdash; one of the three posts named in her [2017 obituary](https://www.mariettatimes.com/obituaries/2017/05/terrie-lee-bain/) (the others were North Hills Elementary and Harmar Elementary).

The companion photograph &mdash; [her reading Star Wars to small Chuck on a couch](/archive/terrie-reading-to-chuck-c1984/) &mdash; is the home version of this same person at this same work.
