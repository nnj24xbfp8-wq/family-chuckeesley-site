**A labeled outdoor reunion portrait of the extended Eesley family**, c. mid-to-late 1970s. The original print carries a **typed caption box at the bottom-left identifying every figure left to right**, and a **second caption box at the bottom-right listing the eight Charles Leonard and Lillie Dale Eesley children with precise birth and death dates** — a piece of structured family documentation embedded in the artifact itself.

## The cast — left to right per the caption

> *Helen "Little Helen" Burnes Eesley; Katie Burnes (Helen Burnes' daughter); Mary Eesley Bean, Donald Eesley; Roberta Burnes (Helen Burnes' daughter); Anne Eesley; Peggy McMaster Eesley; Mafry Smith Hyatt (daughter of Lilly Chenoweth Eesley's sister, Dr. Sciota "Ota" Chenoweth Smith); Wilbur Chenoweth Eesley; Helen "Big Helen" Eesley (Len's wife); Peggy Yeoman Eesley; Len Eesley*

Three generations of the Charles Leonard Eesley descendants in one frame:

**Charles Leonard's surviving children present** (four of the eight):
- **[Mary Eesley Bean](/family/mary-eesley-bean/)** — the family historian, author of the [1985 Eesley Family History](/docs/eesley-family-history-1985/)
- **[Donald Stewart Eesley](/family/don-eesley/)** — "Uncle Donald"
- **[Wilbur "Will" Chenoweth Eesley](/family/wilbur-eesley/)** — Chuck's grandfather, the architect
- **[Helen Louise "Little Helen" Eesley Burnes](/family/helen-burnes/)** — the youngest sister, born after the family's move to Bexley
- **[Leonard David "Len" Eesley](/family/leonard-david-eesley/)** — "Uncle Len," the eldest of the eight

**The Charles Leonard children NOT present** in the photograph: [Dale Dudley](/family/dale-eesley/) (d. 1939 Black Lake), [Lyle](/family/lyle-eesley/) (d. 1942 Cabanatuan), and Jean (d. as a small child).

**Spouses of the surviving siblings**:
- **[Margaret "Peggy" McMaster Eesley](/family/margaret-mcmaster-eesley/)** — Will's wife
- **Helen "Big Helen" Eesley** — Len's wife (a different Helen from Will's sister "Little Helen")
- **Peggy Yeoman Eesley** — Donald's wife (a different Peggy from Will's wife)

**The next generation** (Charles Leonard's grandchildren):
- **Anne Eesley** — Will and Peggy's daughter (Chuck's aunt)
- **Katie Burnes** — Helen Burnes's daughter (Chuck's first cousin once removed)
- **[Roberta Burnes](/family/roberta-burnes/)** — Helen Burnes's daughter (Chuck's first cousin once removed; the keeper of the 1940s Charles Leonard correspondence)

**A visiting cousin from the Chenoweth line**:
- **Mafry Smith Hyatt** — daughter of **Dr. Sciota "Ota" Chenoweth Smith**, [Lillie Dale Chenoweth Eesley's older sister](/family/scioto-mafry-chenoweth/). **This caption is the family's confirmation that Sciota Chenoweth herself was the medical doctor** the family memory recalled — *"daughter of Lilly Chenoweth Eesley's sister, Dr. Sciota 'Ota' Chenoweth Smith."* The "Dr." in front of Sciota's name in the caption is the family's definitive documentation that the family's first-female-MD memory points to her.

## The caption box on the Charles Leonard children

The second caption box at the bottom-right of the print carries the precise dates:

> *Charles Leonard Eesley (26 Sep 1879 to 9 Oct 1972)*
>
> *Lilly Dale Chenoweth (5 Aug 1877 to 1969)*
>
> *Children of Charles Leonard and Lilly Chenoweth Eesley:*
>
> *Leonard Eesley (19 Nov 1904 to Jan 1976)*
>
> *Dale Eesley (5 Aug 1906 to 14 Jul 1939)*
>
> *Donald Eesley (24 Sep 1908 to May 1975)*
>
> *Wilbur Eesley (6 Oct 1910 to 2 Jun 1986)*
>
> *Mary Eesley Bean (25 Aug 1913 to 23 Jul 2002)*
>
> *Lyle Eesley (Jun 1917 to 25 Jul 1942)*
>
> *Helen Eesley Burnes (16 Jan 1924 to 1 Jun 2000)*
>
> *Jean Eesley (1926—1930)*

**Several pieces of new biographical data this caption surfaces for the archive:**

1. **Leonard David Eesley** — precise birth: **19 November 1904** (the archive had just "1904"); death **January 1976** (the archive had "1976").
2. **Mary Eesley Bean** — **death date 23 July 2002** (the archive's Bean register did not give a death date; she died at almost 89, fifteen years after publishing the *Eesley Family History*).
3. **Helen Louise Eesley Burnes** — **death date 1 June 2000** (the archive had just "1924–" without a death year).
4. **Jean Eesley** — **dates 1926–1930** — died at age **four**, not the age 12 the archive currently has on her [Jean Goldie Eesley page](/family/jean-goldie-eesley/) (which gave 1912–1924). The labeled photo caption is the family's record; Charlie's [1965 autobiography](/docs/charles-eesley-12th-grade-autobiography-1965/) said *"My Aunt Jean died from a childhood accident when she was five years old"* — consistent with the 1926–1930 reading. The 1912–1924 dates in the archive appear to be incorrect and need correction.
5. **Lyle Eesley** — caption says *"Jun 1917"* (the archive has him as 12 August 1916 per Mary Bean's 1985 register corrected against the FamilySearch GEDCOM). Three slightly different sources for Lyle's birth date now: Bean's 1985 register (1914), the GEDCOM (12 Aug 1916), and this labeled caption (Jun 1917). The GEDCOM-treated value remains authoritative for now; the caption is documented as a third reading.
6. **Lillie Dale Chenoweth Eesley** — caption says *"1969"* (the archive has January 1970). May be a typo; reconciliation queued.

## Why this artifact is so consequential

The photograph and its caption box together are the **single richest documentary frame in the Eesley-side family-portraiture layer of this archive**. They:

- Provide the **only image in this archive of Mafry Smith Hyatt** — the Chenoweth-cousin link to Dr. Sciota Chenoweth Smith
- Provide what is probably the **only labeled image in the archive of Leonard David "Len" Eesley** in adulthood
- Document the **three Eesley aunts and Big Helen and Peggy Yeoman** as the spouses of the surviving siblings
- Confirm Sciota Chenoweth as the medical doctor of family memory
- Provide precise dates for four siblings that the archive's GEDCOM and Bean register either lacked or had wrong
