A color photograph of **a family group in the living room at [108 Hillcrest Drive](/family/betty-davis/)** &mdash; the home of [Betty Jean Davis Wolfe](/family/betty-davis/) and [Norman Graham Wolfe](/family/norman-graham-wolfe/) &mdash; confirmed by Chuck Eesley 2026. The wood-paneled walls, the built-in bookshelves, and the fireplace are the Hillcrest interior Chuck remembers from holiday dinners and sick-day visits across his childhood.

This is the **same room** as the [2011 Thanksgiving photograph](/archive/norm-betty-wolfe-thanksgiving-2011/), seen at a different angle and on a different occasion &mdash; another documentary frame of the **108 Hillcrest holiday-host pattern** Chuck has named on Betty's and Norm's pages.

Cast queued for Chuck's identification.
