A large outdoor family group portrait taken at the [Highland Ridge Road property](/places/highland-ridge-road-marietta/), c. 1979–1985. About **thirteen figures** are arranged on a grassy slope in casual summer clothes — multiple generations, smiling and informal. The frame reads as a family-reunion-scale gathering on a summer afternoon at the property Will Eesley the architect designed and built for his own family.

A version of this photograph that identifies every figure by name exists in the family archive. Chuck Eesley will add the labeled scan to this archive in a future pass; when it lands, this page will be rewritten with the full cast and the family-relationship structure the group portrait captures.

Its scale (thirteen-plus figures) suggests it captures more than just the immediate four-Eesley-children-of-Will family — possibly a Will-and-Peggy anniversary, a milestone family event, or a multi-branch summer reunion at the property.
