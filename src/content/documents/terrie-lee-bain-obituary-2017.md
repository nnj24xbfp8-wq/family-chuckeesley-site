The published **obituary of Terrie Lee (Wildermuth) Bain** &mdash; **Chuck Eesley's mother** &mdash; following her death **Tuesday, 16 May 2017, at her residence in Marietta**, age 68. This obituary closes the arc opened a year before by [Charlie Eesley's eulogy and 26 November 2015 death](/family/charles-eesley/), and the half-decade before that by [her father Robert Earl Wildermuth's death](/family/robert-earl-wildermuth/), reframing the story this archive has been telling around her: **the lifelong elementary-school teacher** of Marietta whose chosen work was children's literacy, the **traveling Air Force daughter** who landed in Marietta as her permanent home, and the **devoted aunt-and-niece** whose obituary explicitly names her closeness to Betty and Norm Wolfe.

## What the obituary establishes

**Birth:** *"She was born on January 13, 1949 in Oakland, CA to the late Robert and Dorothy Marie Davis Wildermuth."* Terrie was born **in Oakland, California** &mdash; **not Ohio** &mdash; reflecting Robert Earl Wildermuth's then-active military posting on the West Coast (he was a Navy and later Air Force flight surgeon). The Oakland birthplace marks her as a **Cold-War-era military child**, with the family's Marietta home base reached later.

**Early life of travel:** *"Terrie traveled the U.S. and Japan until graduating high school."* &mdash; Terrie's childhood and adolescence followed her father's Air Force postings across **the United States and Japan**. The Japan posting is a previously-undocumented detail. She graduated high school somewhere in that traveling-family arc, then came back to Marietta for college.

**Education:** Marietta College, then University of Maryland. Bachelor's and Master's degrees in **early childhood education**, plus many other certifications including, most recently, a one-year Master Teaching Certification program. Terrie kept developing her teaching credentials her whole working life.

**Teaching career — twenty-three years:** *"Terrie was a school teacher for the Marietta City School District for twenty-three years."* Her teaching positions are named explicitly:

- **Children's librarian at Marietta Public Library**
- **Teacher at North Hills Elementary**
- **Title I teacher at Harmar Elementary** &mdash; teaching at the same Harmar neighborhood where her father Robert Earl had grown up at [413 Harmar Street](/places/413-harmar-street-marietta/).
- **Safety Town instructor** in summers for many years.

**The Wolfe closeness named outright:** *"She loved entertaining visiting family and was a close relative to Betty and Norm Wolfe."* &mdash; the obituary itself **names her closeness to Betty and Norm Wolfe**, the 108 Hillcrest Drive holiday hosts of Chuck's childhood (the [Davis-line aunt and uncle](/family/betty-davis/)). This is the family's own confirmation, in print, of the warmth this archive has tried to render.

**Husband (Benjamin F. Bain II) — fifteen-year marriage:** *"In addition to her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband of 15 years, Benjamin F. Bain II (12/21/2014)."* Terrie's husband **Benjamin F. Bain II** died **21 December 2014** &mdash; just over **two years before** Terrie's own 2017 death, and **eleven months before** her brother Charlie Eesley's 26 November 2015 death. The fifteen-year marriage places its start c. 1999. The Bain name is Terrie's second-married name; her first marriage to Charlie Eesley produced their son Chuck and ended before this second marriage.

**Sole survivor among her children:** *"She is survived by her son, Charles 'Chuck' Eesley, Ph.D. (Lijie) of Stanford, CA."* The obituary names **Chuck and Lijie** (his wife) **of Stanford, California** &mdash; Chuck's settled life by 2017, sixteen years into his time on the West Coast.

**Siblings — the full Wildermuth sibling roster:**

- **Sandra Wildermuth Clement** of Atlanta, GA
- **Debra Wildermuth Massaro** (husband Angelo, deceased)
- **Robert E. Wildermuth, Jr.** (wife Dawn) of Venice, FL

This is the **full surviving-sibling list** at the time of Terrie's death &mdash; the four-sibling Wildermuth set (Sandy, Terrie, Debbie, Bob Jr.), with Terrie being one of two who pre-deceased the others.

**Nieces and nephews:**

- Nieces: **Briana Massaro and Sarah Wildermuth**
- Nephews: **Gian Carlo Massaro, Trey Wildermuth, and Thomas Wildermuth**

Five next-generation Wildermuth-and-Massaro cousins to Chuck. The Massaro branch (through Debbie and Angelo) produced Briana and Gian Carlo; the Wildermuth branch (through Bob Jr. and Dawn) produced Sarah, Trey, and Thomas.

## What the obituary closes

Terrie's death **closed her generation's central thread** in this archive. The Wolfe-and-Davis household network that hosted her childhood holiday dinners (Betty, Norm), the Marietta College arc that brought her to her husband and Faye Angel's Book Store, the Air Force traveling-family upbringing that took her to Japan, and the teaching career across Marietta's elementary schools that closed her working life &mdash; all of them sit in this one obituary's paragraphs. Within **eleven months in 2014–2015** the family lost Charlie (26 Nov 2015), Norm (9 Jan 2015), Betty (18 Jul 2015), and Ben Bain (21 Dec 2014); within **another seventeen months** Terrie followed.

Memorial service: **Saturday 8 July 2017 at 2:00 PM at Hadley Funeral Reno Chapel, 1021 Pike St., Marietta**.
