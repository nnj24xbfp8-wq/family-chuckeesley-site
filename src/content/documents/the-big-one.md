This document contains two interleaved threads: the **mission log** (dates, targets, weather, anti-aircraft, losses) and a **crew roster** of the ten men on the B-24 with Robert Earl. The mission log covers November 1944 through May 1945, operating from forward airfields in the western Pacific — Biak, Leyte, and Mindoro. The crew portraits and short bios that follow the missions include navigator Robert Earl himself, the MIT-educated pilot Van Valen, the radio operator "Fuzzy" Kuszmaul, the Texan tail gunner and crew photographer Jesse Murdock, and the other men of the ten-man crew.
