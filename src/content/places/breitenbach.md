Breitenbach, in the Rhineland-Palatinate, is where Catherina Boeshar was born in 1840 before emigrating to Marietta and marrying Johann Michael Wildermuth. Robert Earl planned a side trip here in 1993 and didn't make it. Chuck retraced the rest of the route in his own generation and didn't make it either. Until one of us does, this page stays open: the place we know about, named in the records, photographed by no one in the family.

> A future visit — by Chuck, by his children, by any descendant — closes this chapter. Until then it is held open intentionally.
