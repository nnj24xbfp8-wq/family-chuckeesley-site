The Evangelische Kirche in Rielingshausen, built in 1752, was the parish church of the Wildermuths through their last German generations. Robert Earl visited in 1993 on the family pilgrimage that became "Return to Germany." A church cleaning lady, suspicious of what an American with a camera might do, followed him from pew to pew until he gave up and left without an interior frame. He recorded the visit in words instead.

Twenty-five years later, in 2018, Chuck returned with no shadow watching and finished the photographic record his grandfather couldn't.
